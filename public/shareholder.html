Shareholder

Desert Solar Power is owned by the Tucher Group, a diversified German family office, and the United Green Group, a family owned group of companies with a strategic focus on utility scale renewable energy assets in emerging markets.

Both Family Offices have a long history of operation and successfully completed various projects in the field of Real Estate, IT, Engineering, Environment and Renewables. Being internationally active they both own and manage several companies in different markets. With Desert Solar Power they joint forces in order to profit from their diverse knowledge and experience in the renewable energy market towards projects that go beyond simply producing renewable power and create additional social and economic benefits for the local communities.

Tucher Group

The Tucher Group is a dynamic family office active in the fields engineering, investment and project management. It has successfully invested in various real estate projects, engineering (MuP Group) and IT companies as well as trading companies. International active it possesses a strong knowledge about the markets it operates in, mainly covering Europe, Asia and Africa.

United Green Group

Born from a family office dating back to the early parts of the 20th Century, United Green has consistently invested in industrial, construction and trading activities in a variety of sectors over the years encompassing many borders. Today United Green is recognized as a privately owned strategic investment group with a diversified and dynamic international portfolio.
