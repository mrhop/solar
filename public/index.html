DESERT SOLAR POWER

Desert Solar Power develops, finances, builds, operates, and maintains utility scale solar energy projects, with a focus on the Mongolian market.

Mongolia offers significant potential for energy generation from renewable sources. It faces increasing energy demand that cannot be met by conventional energy sources alone. In recent years, comprehensive legislation has been implemented to create a framework for the promotion of renewable energy infrastructure investments.

The Sainshand solar PV power plant, with an installed capacity of 30 MWp will be the first utility scale solar PV power plant to be connected to the electricity grid in Mongolia.

With an annual electricity production of up to 56,800 MWh, which equals the electricity consumption of about 27,000 households, the Sainshand Solar Park will support the countries development towards a green and environmental friendly energy generation. The project has the potential to save more than 45,000 tons of CO2 emissions per year.
