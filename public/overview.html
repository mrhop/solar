Company Overview

Desert Solar Power develops, finances, builds, operates and maintains utility scale solar energy projects, with a current focus on the Mongolian market. Its main goal is to expand the investment and development in the renewable energy sector and provide customers with long-term electricity supply from clean sources. Desert Solar Power together with its long-term local and international partners is covering all development phases for the successful realization of our projects. The company was established in 2013 and is headquartered in London, UK with a regional branch in Mongolia.
